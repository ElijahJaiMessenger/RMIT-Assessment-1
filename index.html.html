What is a Sysadmin?

Sysadmins build, monitor and maintain servers for their company.

They’re almost always on the backend of IT. they run a number of servers that include the following type of programs: remote desktop/pc/ phone support, server antivirus, desktop antivirus, pgp encryption, ticketing systems, dns, domain controllers, server monitoring systems and accounting software. Software is becoming bigger as hardware is changing.

skills, qualifications and experience required for the position.

the skills are in being able to learn how the tech works (not just enough to follow troubleshooting steps when it stops working) and in being able to make your solutions work.

Project idea

Dog fit collar

Overview - dogfit collar is a data-driven focus for dogs and their owners to get active and stay healthy together. We build collar sensors and software infrastructure that helps humans to take control of their pet’s location, activity, quality of sleep, calorie expenditure, and overall health and behavior. Dogfit collar can also help with your dogs internal health to help validate new drugs, foods, procedures or medical treatments.

Motivation - The motives behind this product is that it's a good way to track your dogs location, activity, quality of sleep, calorie expenditure, and overall health and behavior. And it’s also a good way to shoot data and research for when you send your pet to the vet the veterian has up to date stats that can help with your dogs internal health to help validate for new drugs, foods, procedures or medical treatments. Using Raspberry Pi technology is a cheap and easily assembled solution to this problem.

Description - (dogfit collar) comes in a variety of colors, and a lightweight one-size-fits-all that is unobtrusive even for small dogs the collar is easy to use as it’s just like a normal collar the battery life is about two weeks and it charges very quickly. just take the collar off the dog and the USB port is easily accessible in the collar.

The app - 

 it's free to download and use, since the money is made in selling the device that makes the app worth downloading. linking the app to the device is super easy, and the app's interface is simple and effortless to navigate. you can connect to several different devices, so dogfit collar is practical even if you own multiple dogs, are a dog walker/pet sitter, etc. you can even link your your own fitbit. fill out your dog's profile with details like breed[s], age, sex, weight, and health conditions, then set a daily goal for points. For some examples of goals is which is met with approximately 60-90 minutes of moderate/high exercise or 60 minutes of moderate/high exercise] and 30-60 minutes of low/moderate exercise. this goal is the bare minimum the dogs needs in order to be less anxious, reactive, restless, and destructive.

Tools and Technologies - we’d use Raspberry Pi is a low cost, credit-card sized computer that and use program languages like Python. And use Python to develop web applications. software applications, pages within a web browser, the shells of operating systems.We’d also use Appery.io to develop the app for smartphones. 

Skills Required - The skills required to develop this product and the tasks that needed to be completed is. Someone who is knowledgeable in using python to develop the code for the collar someone whos continent in app making design to design the app for IOS, android etc, Someone who can design me construct a collar that is well design using Raspberry Pi Technology and someone who to sell and gain investors to fund the project.

Outcome - The outcome of this project would be to have a fully developed website that would show all the current and developing features, and a prototype of the collar to show the design and the different variety you can buy, tested features for example a rough draft on how it monitors your dogs activities, and how it can be uploaded to a cloud server where the dated can be stored and accessed from a tap or click of a button.
